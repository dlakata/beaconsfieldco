About Beaconsfield

Beaconsfield is a holding company that invests in and builds great businesses. We are passionate about the ideas and teams we work with and are committed to adding value as both investors and operators.

As entrepreneurs, we seek to capitalize on underserved markets and alternative business models. We prefer building sustainable businesses that are extremely customer-centric. For the foreseeable future, we plan to re-invest all profits back into our businesses.

As investors, we are hands on partners and are in it for the long term. We help you identify and meet your strategic goals and help optimize and automate things so you can focus on what is truly important.
